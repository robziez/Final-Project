Shining Tiara Jewelry

Shining Tiara Jewelry partners with the community, charitable organizations, and other volunteer services in order to promote successful fundraising events. With an up-to-date, fully stocked display we bring with us everything necessary to open an exciting and colorful fundraising experience. Along with our exquisite jewelry we supply all of the display tables, chairs, table coverings, display stands, cases, and much more. The display is decorated with our glittering tiaras which are also for sale. We donate 20% of all gross proceeds from the sale of the sponsoring entity (hospitals, corporate charities, medical residences, colleges, schools, assisted-living's, corporate fundraising, and religious organizations). We make it easy for you! - We except Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover card. We also supply professional signage on the day of the fundraising event as well as professional flyers sent out to be posted prior to the event. We supply all the work staff necessary to run the event completely. All you need to do is accept the donation of 20% of all proceeds and use it for the charity it is meant for! If you are interested in scheduling a fundraising event with us, we'd love to hear from you! Please refer to our contact page.
